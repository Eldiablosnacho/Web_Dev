When did tacos first appear in the India?

Tacos first appeared in India when the American fast food chain Taco Bell opened its first outlet in Bangalore in 2010.
Explanation: Tacos are originally from Mexico and were not a part of Indian cuisine until the introduction of Taco Bell franchises. 2010 . (Source: NBC News )
